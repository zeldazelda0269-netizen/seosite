Questions? We Have Answers.

Clarifying Your Project Concerns

Do you provide source code ownership?

Yes. Once final payment is made, you own 100% of the intellectual property. We hand over all GitHub repositories, Figma files, and deployment keys.

What happens after the project is launched?

We provide a 30-day post-launch warranty period for bug fixes. Following that, we offer monthly maintenance retainers for continuous updates and security.

How do you handle project communication?

We use a dedicated Slack channel or WhatsApp group for daily communication. We also have weekly video calls via Zoom/Google Meet to demonstrate sprint progress.

Which platform is best for my business?

It depends on your scale. Shopify is best for retail; WordPress is best for content-rich SEO sites; Custom MERN stack is best for unique SaaS business logic.

Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect information such as your name, email, and phone number only when you contact us via WhatsApp or email. This data is used solely for the purpose of consulting on your project.

2. Data Usage

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. It is stored securely on our internal CRM to maintain project records and provide future customer support.

3. Cookies

Our website uses basic analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our content. You can disable these via your browser settings.

4. Compliance

We adhere to international data protection standards (GDPR/CCPA) for our global clients. You may request your data to be deleted at any time by emailing us at naqeeburrehman001@gmail.com.
